Provenance & Integrity
As a bona fide dealer in antiquarian books, Peter is a member of both the Australia and New Zealand Association of Antiquarian Booksellers (ANZAAB) and the International League of Antiquarian Booksellers (ILAB).

You can be assured of the provenance and integrity of any vintage books. We guarantee the authenticity of all items, provide accurate and honest descriptions highlighting any defects, and always operate a transparent pricing policy.

The Vault

Total Inventory: ~20,000 Volumes

Antiquarian books are usually valued more as physical objects rather than purely for the content; consideration is made to the edition, the quality of printing and illustrations, or the extravagance and care taken in the binding.

Covering a broad range of subjects and time periods, you will find real antiquities such as the highly desirable Carey’s New and Correct English Atlas (1787), alongside books of great interest to the general reader. Whether you are interested in Aboriginal art, aviation, history of the Pacific, travel, or classic literature, we will be sure to have something of interest to you.
